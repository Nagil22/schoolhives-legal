SCHOOL HIVE - Account and Data Deletion Request

This page explains how to request the deletion of your account and associated personal data from the SCHOOL HIVE platform, operated by RIXIN SOFTWARE TECHNOLOGY COMPANY LIMITED.

How to Request Deletion

Please follow the correct path based on your user role:

1. For Students, Parents, and School Staff

Your account is managed by your educational institution. To request deletion:

  1. Contact the administrator of your school/institution directly within the SCHOOL HIVE app.
  2. Submit your account deletion request to them.
  3. The school administrator will then process your request in accordance with the institution's policies.

2. For School or Institution Administrators

To request the deletion of your entire school account and all associated data:

  1. Email our support team directly at: support@rixings.com.
  2. Use the subject line: "School Account Deletion Request".
  3. In the email, please provide your school name and the registered admin email address for verification.

What Data We Delete

Upon valid request, we will delete the following personal data associated with the account:

  • User profile information (full name, profile photo)
  • School/institution information linked to the account
  • User-generated content within the platform
  • Access credentials and session data

Data Retention Note

Please note that some anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for ongoing analysis and service improvement. Additionally, we may be required to retain certain transaction data for a legally mandated period (e.g., for financial record-keeping), but this will not be used for any other purpose.

For more general information, please see our full Privacy Policy.
