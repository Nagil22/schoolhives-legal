
1. PRIVACY POLICY
SCHOOL HIVE
Last updated: 7 January 2026
1.1 Introduction
SCHOOL HIVE is an education-focused platform operated by RIXIN SOFTWARE TECHNOLOGY COMPANY LIMITED (“we”, “our”, “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information when users access the SCHOOL HIVE mobile application and related services.
By using SCHOOL HIVE, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1.2 Information We Collect
We collect only information necessary to provide the service.
1.2.1 Information Provided by Users
* Full name
* Profile photograph
* School or institution-related information
* Account credentials (stored in hashed and encrypted form)
* User-generated content submitted within the platform
  
1.2.2 Automatically Collected Information
* Device information
* IP address
* Usage logs and analytics
* Session and interaction data
1.2.3 Payments
When payments are made, they are processed by Paystack. We do not store card or bank details on our servers.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
* Email address
* Phone number
* Biometric identifiers
* Precise location data

1.4 Messaging and Communication
SCHOOL HIVE provides real-time communication using a custom socket-based messaging system (Socket.IO) hosted on our own infrastructure. We do not use third-party push notification services for messaging delivery.

1.5 How We Use Information
We use collected data to:
* Create and manage user accounts
* Provide school-related services and communication
* Enable real-time messaging
* Process payments
* Monitor system usage and performance
* Improve platform reliability and security
* Comply with legal obligations

1.6 Account Management and Deletion
1.6.1 Students, Parents, and Staff
Account deletion requests must be initiated through the school administration of the institution associated with the account.
1.6.2 Schools and Institutions
Schools or institutions wishing to discontinue use of SCHOOL HIVE may request account removal by contacting:
support@rixings.com

1.7 Data Sharing
We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared only:
* With school administrators authorized by the institution
* With payment processors (Paystack) strictly for transaction handling
* When required by law or lawful authority

1.8 Data Security
We implement administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

1.9 Children’s Privacy
SCHOOL HIVE is intended for users aged 13 years and above, operating under school supervision.

1.10 Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be published on this page.

1.11 Contact
For privacy-related questions or concerns:
Email: support@rixings.com
